Pre-release 7.0 (revision 1)

Hello, everyone! I am excited to announce our FIRST preview of SailrMC, for all to use! I've been working on this for a few days now, so this is very exciting.

Shortlist of changes:

  • Removed DRM from offline accounts
  • (mostly) rebranded the launcher
  • Use our own metaservers

  • Use our own RSS feed for news and more

This is the first step in my plans for this launcher, so stay tuned!
